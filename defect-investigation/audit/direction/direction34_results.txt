Conclusion:
- If the normal birth_defect/MutatePiece path applies no-eyes, no-eyebrows, or no-ears as GON block -2, the save should contain 0xFFFFFFFE in T.
- Alaya's explicit no-ears defect behaves that way: ear_L/ear_R T part IDs are 0xFFFFFFFE.
- Whommie and Bud do not behave that way: their affected T part IDs remain ordinary base-shape IDs and all neighboring serialized fields are zero except texture echo.
- Therefore Whommie/Bud missing detections are probably not coming from the direct body-part mutation application path.
